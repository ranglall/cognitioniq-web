Process

How It Works

A streamlined, four-step process that delivers objective cognitive data — fast.

01

Baseline Assessment

Participants complete our 5–10 minute assessment using a VR headset, following visual targets with their eyes. No prior training required. The process is comfortable, non-invasive, and clinically rigorous.

02

Secure Data Storage

Their cognitive data is securely stored and immediately accessible through your organization's private CognitionIQ portal. All data is encrypted end-to-end and HIPAA-compliant.

03

Post-Event Reassessment

If a potential injury occurs, the participant retakes the same 5–10 minute assessment. The consistency of the protocol ensures results are directly comparable to the baseline.

04

Algorithm-Driven Decision

Our specialized algorithm compares both tests and delivers clear, data-backed return-to-play recommendations directly to your portal — removing guesswork from high-stakes decisions.

The entire process is standardized, objective, and fast.

About

Our Story

Where the mission to revolutionize brain health began.

CognitionIQ was founded on a simple but urgent observation: the tools used to assess brain health have not kept pace with our understanding of the brain itself.

For decades, cognitive assessment has relied on subjective reporting — asking patients how they feel, watching for behavioral cues, and interpreting inconsistent symptom checklists. In high-stakes environments like sports medicine, military readiness, and occupational safety, this approach is not just insufficient. It can be dangerous.

We set out to change that. Drawing on years of federally funded research in oculometrics — the scientific measurement of eye movements as a proxy for brain function — our team developed a proprietary algorithm capable of extracting meaningful cognitive data from a simple 5–10 minute eye-tracking assessment conducted through a standard VR headset.

Our Mission

To replace subjective symptom reporting with measurable cognitive data — making brain function assessment standardized, trusted, and accessible in every environment where neurological health matters.

The science is clear: eye movements are a direct window into neural function. They cannot be coached, suppressed, or faked. By building our platform on this foundation, we created a tool that is not only more accurate than traditional methods — it is fundamentally more honest.

Today, CognitionIQ is working with partners across memory care, sports medicine, occupational safety, military readiness, and clinical research — bringing objective cognitive measurement to the moments when it matters most.

We are still early. The science of oculometric cognitive assessment is advancing rapidly, and so are we. If you believe that brain health deserves better tools, we'd love to hear from you.
